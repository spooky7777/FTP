Our services

Enhancing Your Vehicle’s Performance

We offer a full range of services, from routine maintenance and diagnostics to complete restorations and performance upgrades. Our team is dedicated to bringing out the best in your vehicle, ensuring safety, reliability, and enhanced performance.

  • Custom Exhaust Work

    Custom exhausts are our specialty. We create tailored exhaust solutions that maximize performance, deliver a unique sound. Our team brings expertise and precision to every exhaust system, making sure it fits your vehicle perfectly and meets your performance goals.

  • Brake Services

    We offer comprehensive brake services, from repairs and replacements to high-performance upgrades. Our team ensures your brakes are safe, responsive, and suited to your driving needs.

  • Engine Swaps

    We handle complete engine swaps, giving your vehicle a new heart with improved power and performance. Our team manages every detail, ensuring a precise fit and optimal functionality for a seamless upgrade.

  • Suspension Services

    Our suspension services provide a smoother, more controlled ride with custom solutions tailored to your driving style. From upgrades to repairs, we enhance handling, stability, and comfort for a superior driving experience.

  • Forced Induction

    Boost your vehicle’s power with our forced induction services. We specialize in installing and tuning superchargers and turbochargers to deliver maximum horsepower and torque. Our team ensures a precise setup for reliable, high-performance gains.

  • Wheels & Tires

    We offer a wide selection of wheels and tires to enhance your vehicle’s appearance and performance. From custom wheels to tire replacements, our team ensures the perfect fit for improved handling, safety, and style.

  • Clutch Upgrades

    Our clutch upgrade services are designed to handle more power and provide smoother shifting. Whether you're upgrading for performance or durability, our team ensures a perfect fit to enhance your driving experience and keep your transmission performing at its best.

  • General Repairs

    We provide reliable general repair services for all makes and models. From routine maintenance to complex fixes, our team ensures your vehicle stays in top condition, keeping you safe and on the road.
