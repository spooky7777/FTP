We're a Performance & Mechanic Shop

For over two decades, we’ve specialized in elevating vehicle performance with top-quality parts, expert service, and personalized attention. From routine maintenance to custom upgrades, our team is dedicated to helping you get the most out of your car on the street or track.

Mission & Vision

Full Throttle Performance Mission & Vision

Our skilled professionals handle everything from diagnostic tests and fluid services to engine replacements, oil changes, and complete vehicle overhauls. We’re committed to providing reliable, high-quality service for every vehicle.

  • Mission Statement

    Our mission is to deliver exceptional customer service, continually develop our team, and embrace environmentally responsible practices. We aim to exceed expectations with every repair and interaction.

  • Our Vision

    To lead the automotive industry by setting new standards in customer satisfaction and innovation. We are committed to excitement through forward-thinking solutions, effective problem-solving, and consistently surpassing our customers' expectations.
